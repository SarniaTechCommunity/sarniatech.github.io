The Sarnia Tech Community is a member-driven group. We hold both regular and irregular events that are open to the public. Most of these events operate without an entry fee. If you would like to help support our group, you can reach out through any of the programs below:

Cash or Cheque Donation

Cheques made out to the "Sarnia Tech Community" can be provided to Len Payne at any regular or irregular event. Len takes cash too. If you would like a receipt, Len can provide you with one, but as we are not a registered charity, we cannot provide a charitable tax receipt.
